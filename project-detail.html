Doma Tower — Business Bay

Ongoing
Dubai, UAE Real Estate Est. Completion: Q4 2025
Main View — Doma Tower

Project Overview

Doma Tower is a landmark 42-storey Grade-A commercial skyscraper positioned at the heart of Business Bay, Dubai's thriving financial and business district. The tower delivers 128,000 sqm of premium Gross Floor Area, comprising world-class office suites, a vibrant retail podium, and an exclusive private club on levels 38–40.

The architecture — designed by global firm Zaha Hadid Architects — features a distinctive twisting facade that maximises natural light while reducing solar gain by 40% compared to conventional towers. The building targets LEED Platinum and WELL Gold certifications upon completion.

With over 85% of office space pre-leased to anchor tenants including two Fortune 500 companies, Doma Tower represents one of the region's most successful pre-leasing campaigns in recent years.
