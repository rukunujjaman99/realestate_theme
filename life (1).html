What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren't words on a wall. They are the criteria we use to hire, promote, and make every significant decision at Doma.

01
Uncompromising Integrity
We have walked away from profitable transactions that conflicted with our ethical standards. Integrity is our most valuable and most protected asset.
02
Excellence in Execution
Good enough is not a Doma standard. From a board presentation to a construction joint, we insist on the same level of precision and craft.
03
People-First Leadership
Our leaders are measured on how well they grow the people around them. Titles open doors; character determines whether people want to follow.
04
Long-Horizon Thinking
We build for 50-year legacy, not quarterly returns. Every decision is tested against whether it would make us proud in 2045, not just in 2025.
05
Inclusive by Design
Our diversity of nationality, background, and perspective is a strategic advantage. We actively design our culture to include — not merely tolerate — difference.
06
Sustainability as Duty
Environmental responsibility is not a marketing claim at Doma — it is an engineering requirement. Every project must improve on its predecessor's sustainability metrics.
