Doma Launches Doma Residences: 214 Ultra-Luxury Units in Business Bay Fully Subscribed at Launch

June 12, 2025
4 min read
12,481 views
Dubai, UAE

Doma Holding's most anticipated residential launch of 2025 — Doma Residences, Business Bay — sold out across all 214 units in 72 hours, achieving AED 1.6 billion in combined sales value and drawing investors from 19 countries to an exclusive launch event at Atlantis The Palm.

The sellout represents Doma Holding's fastest-ever launch absorption rate, surpassing the previous record set by Palm Lagoon Residences in 2022. The development, comprising a 47-storey tower with studios through to 5-bedroom sky villas, achieved an average selling price of AED 2,800 per square foot — a new benchmark for Business Bay, which traditionally commands AED 2,100–2,400/sqft for comparable grade product.

The Launch Event

Over 400 high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and institutional investors attended the VIP launch at Atlantis The Palm's Saffron Ballroom on June 10th. Guests were given an immersive AR walkthrough of all unit types and access to a real-time reservation portal, with units being reserved at a pace of approximately three per hour throughout the evening.

"Doma Residences represents the clearest signal yet that Business Bay has graduated from a commercial hub to a genuine luxury residential destination. The depth of international demand we saw in 72 hours reflects both the quality of the product and the strength of the Doma brand globally."

— Abdullah Al-Doma, Chairman & CEO, Doma Holding

The launch attracted particular interest from European buyers (32% of reservations), who cited Dubai's tax-neutral environment and the strength of the euro against the dirham as key factors. Indian and South Asian investors accounted for 28% of reservations, with the remainder split across GCC, Russian, and other international buyers.

The Doma Residences launch event at Atlantis The Palm, June 10, 2025

Project Specifications

Doma Residences will deliver 214 units across three distinct residence types: Standard Residences (floors 8–30), Premium Residences (floors 31–42), and Sky Villas (floors 43–47). All units feature floor-to-ceiling glazing, smart home integration by Crestron, and direct connectivity to the Dubai Metro's Business Bay station via an enclosed climate-controlled walkway.

Key Amenities

  • Sky Club on level 44 — residents-only lounge, co-working, and dining
  • 50-metre infinity pool with panoramic Business Bay Canal views
  • Private cinema with 22 seats and streaming concierge
  • Full-floor wellness centre with gym, spa, and meditation studio
  • 24-hour concierge, valet, and dedicated building manager
  • 5-star hotel-service optional package through Doma's hospitality arm

Delivery & Investment Outlook

Doma Residences is scheduled for handover in Q4 2027, with construction commencing on July 1, 2025. Investors on the original launch price can anticipate capital appreciation of 18–25% by handover, based on current trajectory modelling — though Doma advises all investors to conduct independent investment analysis appropriate to their individual circumstances.

A 70/30 payment plan structure (70% over construction, 30% on handover) was offered to all launch buyers. A small number of units were reserved for institutional cash buyers at a 3% premium, all of which were taken within 48 hours.
